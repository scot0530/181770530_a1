Professor Florence Nightingale

Florence is an English social reformer and statistician, as well as the main creator of modern nursing. She first served managing nurses in the war, as she became an icon of Victorian culture, knows as "The Lady with the Lamp" because she would often work the night shifts and thus used a lamp to illuminate her and her patients.

Some believe that her Crimean War achievements were overstated by the media outlets such as newspapers and word of mouth, but even now many agree that she held an important role for developing the reputation of women's roles.
